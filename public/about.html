About Me

Hi, I’m Divya—a web developer with a passion for creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences. I specialize in designing and developing websites that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional, user-friendly, and optimized for performance.

My journey into web development began with a curiosity for technology and a love for problem-solving. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, working with various frameworks, programming languages, and tools to build websites that make a difference. Beyond coding, I’m an avid reader and a firm believer in sustainable living. That’s what led me to create Rebook Reread—a platform dedicated to promoting the joy of second-hand books. I wanted to bridge the gap between technology and sustainability by developing a space where book lovers can buy, sell, and share pre-loved books, reducing waste while fostering a strong reading community.

Through Rebook Reread, I’ve combined my technical expertise with my personal mission: to make books more accessible and contribute to a more eco-conscious world. Whether you’re here to explore second-hand books or you’re interested in web development, I’d love to connect and share ideas. Let’s build, innovate, and keep the pages turning—both in books and in the digital world!
